Project Proposal

After discussion on Data Visualization in first few lectures of class, our team came up with different ideas regarding project proposal such as fake news detection, cyber security and health related issues. But after going much deeper in these fields we finally decided to make an intersection between health sector,our current situation and data driven design. So we finally decided to take problem regarding supply chain optimization for COVID-19 Vaccine.Our aim is to create a machine learning model which will be able to optimize the supply for limited number of COVID Vaccines for initial stages of supply.
